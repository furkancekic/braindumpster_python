Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

Welcome to Braindumpster! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

TL;DR: We collect minimal data (email, name, tasks), use Firebase for secure storage, never sell your data, and you can delete your account anytime.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and profile information
  • Brain Dumps & Tasks: Your voice recordings, text entries, and generated tasks
  • User Preferences: App settings and notification preferences

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, crash reports, and performance metrics
  • Voice Data: Audio recordings when you use voice input (processed and deleted after conversion)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our AI-powered task extraction service
  • Process voice and text brain dumps into organized tasks
  • Send you smart reminders and notifications
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Improve our AI models and app features
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. AI & Voice Processing

When you use voice input:

  • Audio is sent to Google Gemini AI for speech-to-text conversion
  • The audio is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Only the transcribed text and extracted tasks are saved
  • All AI processing is encrypted in transit

4. Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard security measures:

  • Firebase: All data stored on Google Cloud Platform with enterprise-grade security
  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication with secure token-based access
  • No Third-Party Selling: We never sell your data to third parties

5. Data Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • Service Providers: Google Cloud (Firebase), Google Gemini AI - only as necessary to provide services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing requires your explicit permission

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • Share your tasks or brain dumps with other users
  • Use your data for targeted advertising

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View all your stored information in the app
  • Update Information: Edit your profile and preferences anytime
  • Delete Your Account: Permanently delete your account and all data from Settings
  • Export Data: Request a copy of your data (contact support)
  • Opt-Out of Notifications: Disable push notifications in Settings

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the app:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Scroll to Danger Zone
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

When you delete your account:

  • All tasks, brain dumps, and conversations are permanently deleted
  • Your subscription data is removed
  • Your authentication account is deleted
  • This action is irreversible

8. Children's Privacy

Braindumpster is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to your registered address
  • Updated "Last Updated" date on this page

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@braindumpster.io
  • Website: https://braindumpster.io
Your Trust Matters: We're committed to transparency and protecting your privacy. If you have any questions, we're here to help.
